Common questions

Do I need to know how to code, or use n8n myself?

No. Off-the-shelf workflows come with a full setup guide, and custom systems are handed off fully built and documented. You don't need any automation experience to run either one.

I already use my own tools, calendar, CRM, and so on. Does this replace them?

No. Automations are built to connect to the tools you already use, not replace them. If something you rely on isn't supported out of the box, that's a conversation for the custom systems track.

Is my data safe?

Every integration runs through the official APIs of the platforms involved. Your accounts stay yours, and nothing routes through a third-party server you don't control.

What if what I need isn't covered by a ready-made workflow?

That's exactly what the custom systems track is for. Get in touch and we'll figure out what a workflow built around your day-to-day would look like.
